THE SPACES BETWEEN US,
A MOVING DRAWING,
2017

THE SPACES BETWEEN US WAS CREATED IN REACTION TO A DANCE FOR SENIORS AT THE OPERA HOUSE, GENT.  OBSERVED WAS HOW TWO PERSONS MOVE AS ONE. COMING TOGETHER AND MOVING APART THE SENIORS DANCE IN AN INTIMACY THAT IS RARELY RECREATED IN ANY OTHER KIND OF PUBLIC EVENT.
